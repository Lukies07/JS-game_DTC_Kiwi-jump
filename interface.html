Game Information:

You are the blue square at
the bottom of the level.
The objective of the game is
to get to the purple portal,
doing so means you have
beaten the level.
To control the player use either
WASD or the the arrow keys.
The jump boost increases your jump
strength, allowing you to jump higher.
The speed boost Increases
your speed, this allows you to
travel faster.
The kiwi spirit allows you to
harness the power of picking up
power ups, you need to collect it
before you can pick up
any other power ups.

Important to know:

You keep the buff of the power ups
for the entirity of the level.

You lose all the buffs the
player has and you are put
back to where the player origanlly
was at the start of the level if
you press the restart button.
